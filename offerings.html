Our Offerings

Ultrasound Equipment

We trust the SonoSite mTurbo and similar portable ultrasound systems for their durability and crystal-clear imaging. Perfect for maternal health, emergency diagnostics, and routine screenings in remote locations. Please contact us for a friendly conversation about how we can help meet your ultrasound needs.

Endoscopy Systems & Scopes

Our selection includes the Olympus GIF-160 endoscopy system and a variety of interchangeable scopes designed for gastrointestinal and ENT procedures. These compact and rugged units are ideal for mission clinics seeking reliable visualization tools without excessive bulk.

EKG Machines

We offer easy-to-use EKG devices such as the Zoll M Series, pre-tested and calibrated for accurate readings wherever you serve. These machines are designed for field conditions with minimal maintenance requirements.

X-ray Units

Our portable X-ray machines, including models like the MinXray HF100/200, come with digital imaging panels and are engineered for low-power operation and simple setup. They can be deployed quickly in field hospitals or rural clinics, bringing essential radiography to areas without fixed infrastructure.

All our offerings emphasize portability, straightforward setup, and minimal maintenance. We believe in sharing our expertise—not pushing sales—so you get exactly what you need to serve your community effectively.
