About Med Mission Supplies

Med Mission Supplies was founded with a friendly, mission-driven spirit: to serve clinics and mission hospitals across less fortunate regions. We deliver essential medical tools—like the SonoSite mTurbo ultrasound, Olympus GIF-160 endoscopy systems, Zoll M Series EKG machines, and portable X-ray units—without burdening partners with high costs or complicated logistics. Our goal is simple: to help you focus on patient care while we handle the equipment details.

We believe in building sustainable partnerships through hands-on training and ongoing remote support. By ensuring local teams can maintain and operate devices independently, we help strengthen long-term healthcare capacity. Whether you’re a mission project in need of reliable imaging gear or a rural clinic seeking affordable diagnostics, we’re here to support you with compassion and expertise.
