About Med Mission Supplies

Med Mission Supplies was founded with a friendly, mission-driven spirit: to empower healthcare providers in resource-limited settings. We partner with clinics and mission hospitals in the USA and Africa to deliver essential equipment, from portable ultrasound units to endoscopy systems and EKG machines. By minimizing overhead and leveraging cost-effective refurbished devices, we ensure that every dollar spent maximizes patient impact.

Beyond simply supplying equipment, we offer hands-on training and remote support—because our success comes from building sustainable partnerships. Whether you’re a rural clinic needing diagnostic machines or a mission project setting up an emergency response unit, we’re here to help with friendly service and reliable solutions.
